Quick Rules

  • Strike: choose Sea or Air, click a cell. Miss → Fog token appears.
  • Recon: click a cell to scan a 3×3 area: clears Fog in that area and reports how many occupied spaces are inside.
  • Fog tokens reduce certainty. Recon is the only way to reliably clear them.
  • Air Superiority: if you have more active planes than your opponent, you may ask 1 yes/no question each turn.
  • Win: sink all opponent ships (hit every ship space).
